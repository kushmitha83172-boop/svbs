Biligirirangana Hills
(BR Hills) is a scenic hill range known for its forest reserve and wildlife. It connects the Western and Eastern Ghats, making it rich in biodiversity. The hills offer trekking, wildlife spotting, and spiritual experiences.
Biligirirangana Hills

It is located at a high altitude in the BR Hills. The temple is often covered in mist, creating a magical atmosphere. It is dedicated to Lord Krishna and offers panoramic views of the forest.
